The Walmart Opportunity

Why Walmart.com Is the Smartest Move
Most Brands Aren't Making

Most brands are all-in on Amazon. Walmart.com is growing fast, less crowded, and still has room for early movers to build real advantages. The brands that show up now compound that lead for years.

📈

Fastest-Growing Major Marketplace

Walmart.com has grown marketplace seller revenue by double digits year over year — outpacing Amazon's growth rate in several recent quarters.

🏆

Less Competition. Lower Ad Costs.

Fewer sellers. Lower CPCs. Organic ranking that's actually achievable. If your category is crowded on Amazon, Walmart might be your best-kept secret.

🛍️

A Different Customer Base

Walmart shoppers are a distinct segment — value-driven, loyal, and not fully overlapping with Amazon's audience. It's not cannibalization. It's expansion.

🔗

Online Plus In-Store Integration

Walmart's omnichannel infrastructure means your online presence can drive in-store traffic at massive scale. No other marketplace offers that.

💰

Lower Seller Fees

Walmart's referral fees are competitive — and in many categories lower than Amazon's. More margin on every sale adds up fast.

🚀

WFS: Walmart's Answer to FBA

Walmart Fulfillment Services gets you 2-day shipping, Buy Box favorability, and hands-off logistics. The infrastructure is there. Most brands just haven't tapped it.
