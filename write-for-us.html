Submission Guidelines

Read these before you pitch.

✓
Originality All articles must be original and not published elsewhere — including your own site, Medium, LinkedIn, or any other platform. We do not publish AI-generated content, or content with any AI-generated sections.
✓
Length & quality Articles should be 1,500 words or longer. We prioritize depth and usefulness over length — a tightly written 1,500-word piece beats a padded 3,000-word one every time.
✓
Sources & claims Back up your claims with reliable sources, data, or original research. Opinion is fine; unsupported generalizations are not.
✓
Backlinks You may include up to two links to your own website within the article body. A third link is permitted in your author bio. Anything beyond that will be removed.
✓
Images Only include images you own, have created yourself, have explicit permission to use, or that are copyright-free. Do not submit screenshots from third-party tools without permission.

We don't accept:

  • Paid link insertions or sponsored posts
  • Generic beginner-level content already covered everywhere
  • AI-generated drafts or content with AI-generated sections
  • Posts that are thinly veiled sales pitches
